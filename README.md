### Music to Listen to~Dance to~Blaze to~Pray to~Feed to~Sleep to~Talk to~Grind to~Trip to~Breathe to~Help to~Hurt to~Scroll to~Roll to~Love to~Hate to~Learn Too~Plot to~Play to~Be to~Feel to~Breed to~Sweat to~Dream to~Hide to~Live to~Die to~Go To

~GO TO~ is a collections of albums/songs that you've put off for later. A sort of reading list for music, if you would. Inspired by the acclaimed Bring Me The Horizon album of the same name, ~GO TO~ is a site for personal catalogging of your music taste. Using the Spotify API, we are able to gather all the needed meta data, for display purposes. Additionally, we place markers on the albums indicating thier listened status (Havent listend, in progress, listened), a rating out of 10, and other recomended albums.
